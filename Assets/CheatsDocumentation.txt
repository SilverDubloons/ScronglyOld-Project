To turn cheats on, open the menu and go to settings, then press J and Q down at the same time. They both need to be triggered as being pushed down on the same frame. This may take several tries.

When cheats are on the following work

The bauble button will add 1 of the bauble associated with the integer in the inputfield above the button. See BaubleScript for bauble ints.
The nonstandard card button will add 1 of the nonstandard card associated with the integer in the inputfield above the button. See NonStandardCardScript for card ints.

Holding 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 0, J, Q, K, or A down on the keyboard and clicking on a card will change the card to that rank. This does not change the cards value.
Holding H, D, C, S, or R down on the keyboard and clicking on a card will change the card to that suit

Pressing Z will draw 7 cards
Pressing 1 will add one chip
Pressing Backspace will remove one chip
Pressing V will add 1000 chips
Pressing X will purchase the high card bauble 5001 times
Pressing B will purchase the straight flush bauble 999999999 times
Pressing N will unlock all decks
Pressing M will make current run assume you've played all high tier hands
